100 DarkStation Hyper Light Drifter Critic Review

You owe it to yourself to give Hyper Light Drifter a go.
It’s an incredible game with a wonderful look and feel to every part of it.
It’s beautiful, looking great on the Switch’s screen, and can be surprisingly enveloping as an experience even as you take it to louder, crowded public areas.
Every new area and experience was a delight to me, and I kept taking screenshots all the way through.
It’s a challenge, but every moment was worth it.
